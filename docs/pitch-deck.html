04 / 12

How it works

Frame → Imagine → Surface → Prioritise → De-risk

Frame the bet — name the thing, who it's for, and the one belief it rests on.
Imagine the failure — write the post-mortem headline from six months in the future.
Surface the risks — across four lenses, with an offline engine that suggests the failure modes relevant to your product.
Map & prioritise — an interactive likelihood × impact matrix finds your single riskiest assumption.
Plan to de-risk — an experiment toolbox recommends the cheapest test for each risk.
05 / 12

The four risk lenses

Every product dies one of four deaths.

Desirability

Do people actually want this?

Usability

Can people figure out how to use it?

Feasibility

Can we actually build and run it?

Viability

Does it work for the business?

A curated, keyword-triggered library surfaces the right failure modes per lens — instantly, fully offline.

06 / 12

The output

A pre-mortem you can score and screenshot.

Exposure score

A weighted 0–100 read on how dangerous the unmitigated bet is — top risks carry most of the signal.

Readiness score

How much of that danger you actually have a plan to test, plus a bonus for covering all four lenses.

Then: a shareable link, copy-to-Markdown, and print-to-PDF — so the insight travels.
